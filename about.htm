About Jugal

Hello! I’m Jugal Pakhare, a passionate and self-driven web enthusiast from Maharashtra, India. I'm currently pursuing my B.Tech in Information Technology from Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad.

I’m on a journey to become a full-stack developer by learning through real-world projects and building everything by myself — including this portfolio!

Besides coding, I’m also a content creator. I run a YouTube channel called "Wexplore with Jugal" where I share tech-related content and personal explorations. I’ve grown it to a community of around 3000 subscribers.

I love creating beautiful, functional websites, exploring the latest technologies, and constantly improving myself.
